#### Trainees in the lab work at the interface of neuroscience, physiology, behavior, and computational modeling.

### Postdoctoral scientists
If you are excited to use advanced computational and neuroscience approaches to answer questions with direct translational relevance, we'd love to hear from you! Informal inquiries welcome to abowen5 [at] uw [dot] edu.

### Undergraduates
If you're a driven CS student wanting to develop AI models for real-world benefit, or a curious neuroscience or biology student eager to work with large datasets and modern quantitative methods, reach out about interning in the lab -- we'd love to learn more about your interests!

### What we look for
- Curiosity and motivation
- Comfort with quantitative thinking (or desire to learn)
- Collaborative mindset
