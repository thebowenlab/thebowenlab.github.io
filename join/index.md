#### **Trainees in the lab work at the interface of neuroscience, physiology, behavior, and computational modeling.**

### Postdoctoral scientists
If you are excited to use advanced computational and neuroscience approaches to answer biologically important questions with translational relevance, we would love to hear from you. Informal inquiries welcome to <span class="email-obf">abowen5 [at] uw [dot] edu</span>.

### Undergraduates
If you are a CS student interested in building AI models for real-world biomedical problems, or a neuroscience or biology student eager to work with large datasets and modern quantitative methods, reach out about opportunities to intern in the lab. We would be glad to learn more about your interests!

### What we look for
- Curiosity and motivation
- Comfort with quantitative thinking (or desire to learn)
- Collaborative mindset
